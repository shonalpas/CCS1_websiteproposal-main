THE ISCP SCHOOL GUIDELINES

Attendance

Attend school every day, rain or shine, because we all know that learning only happens inside the classroom, and missing a day will undoubtedly ruin your entire education.

Dress Code

Make sure to follow the ultra-specific dress code that was clearly designed by a fashion expert. After all, what you wear is far more important than what you learn.

Cellphones

Absolutely no cell phones allowed, because we wouldn't want you to access the vast wealth of human knowledge at your fingertips or be reachable by your parents in case of emergencies.

Homeworks

Expect hours of homework every night because your education doesn't end when the school bell rings. Remember, sleep is overrated.

Punctuality

Being one minute late to class is a crime against humanity, and it's essential to ensure that you sprint through the hallways like your life depends on it.

No Talking

Silence is golden, and you should never engage in any form of communication with your classmates, especially during lunch or recess. You're here to work, not socialize.

Hall Passes

Make sure to obtain a hall pass from a teacher to use the restroom, as if you're not already trusted to make that decision responsibly.

Lunch

Enjoy the gourmet cuisine served in the cafeteria, and don't forget to savor every bite of the mystery meat and cardboard pizza.

Lockers

Your locker is your second home, and it's absolutely necessary to stuff it with every single textbook you own, just in case you need to do a spontaneous weightlifting session.

Recess

Playtime is for the weak! Who needs fresh air and physical activity when you can sit in a classroom for eight hours straight?

Remember, this the school guidelines are meant purely for humor and not to reflect the actual objectives of any real educational institution.
