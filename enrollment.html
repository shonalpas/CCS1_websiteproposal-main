ISCP Admission Procedures and Requirements


REQUIREMENTS

1. IQ Test for Pets:
All applicants must submit their pets for an IQ test. Pets with a higher IQ than their owners are given special consideration.

2. Ability to Communicate with Plants:
Prospective students should demonstrate their ability to communicate with plants using advanced botanical sign language. Fluency in conversations with ficus trees is a plus.

3. Extraterrestrial Language Proficiency:
Proficiency in at least one extraterrestrial language is mandatory. Klingon, Vulcan, or Wookiee languages are acceptable options.

4. Thesis on Time Travel Ethics:
Applicants must submit a thesis on the ethical implications of time travel. Extra points if the thesis includes firsthand accounts of time travel adventures.

5. Expertise in Paranormal Investigations:
Candidates with experience in ghost hunting, UFO sightings, or Loch Ness Monster tracking are encouraged to apply. A portfolio of supernatural encounters is required.

6. Mastery of Quantum Cooking:
A cooking demonstration showcasing the ability to prepare meals using quantum physics principles. Bonus points for creating a dish that tastes different in different dimensions.

7. Proficiency in Fictional Sports:
Proficiency in sports from fictional universes, such as Quidditch from Harry Potter or podracing from Star Wars, will be tested. Bonus points for inventing a new sport involving broomsticks and lightsabers.

8. Virtual Reality Diplomacy Skills:
Candidates must demonstrate their ability to resolve international conflicts in a virtual reality setting. Bonus points for negotiating peace treaties between aliens and humans in a simulated universe.

9. Originality in Zombie Apocalypse Survival Plans:
Submit a detailed and original plan for surviving a zombie apocalypse. Points will be awarded for creativity, practicality, and the innovative use of household items as weapons.

10. Mastering the Art of Jedi Mind Tricks:
Candidates must pass a Jedi mind tricks exam, proving their ability to influence the thoughts and actions of others. The use of the Force is strictly prohibited during the exam.

PROCEDURES

Step 1: Telepathic Aptitude Test
To ensure our students are intellectually advanced, we conduct a telepathic aptitude test. Candidates must demonstrate their ability to communicate using only their minds. Don't worry if you've never tried this before; just think really hard about your favorite food, and we'll pick up on it.

Step 2: Synchronized Swimming Audition
Education is not just about the mind; it's about the body too! Candidates must showcase their synchronized swimming skills in a pool filled with academic textbooks. Bonus points if you can recite Shakespearean sonnets while executing a perfect backflip.

Step 3: Creative Excuse Writing
We believe in nurturing creativity, especially when it comes to making excuses. Applicants must submit a list of the most imaginative excuses they can come up with for missing classes or assignments. Points will be awarded for creativity, humor, and believability. Bonus points for including extraterrestrial encounters in your excuses.

Step 4: Pet Interview
Your pet's opinion matters to us! Bring your furry, feathery, or scaly friend for a one-on-one interview with our resident therapy animals. We want to know if your pet thinks you're ready for college. If your goldfish nods in approval, you're in!

Step 5: Pie-Eating Contest
Because why not? A classic pie-eating contest will be held, and contestants will be judged based on their ability to eat pie while discussing complex philosophical concepts. The messier, the better. Extra points for eloquence with a mouthful of blueberry filling.

Step 6: Impersonation Challenge
Show off your acting skills by impersonating famous historical figures, mythical creatures, or even inanimate objects. Bonus points if you can convincingly impersonate a library book whispering secrets to readers.

Step 7: Singing in the Rain
In a nod to the classic movie, candidates must perform a musical number in the pouring rain. Extra points for incorporating algebraic equations into the lyrics or performing a rain dance that accurately predicts the weather for the next month.
